Intelligence

Espionage. There are 4 deployment sites: civilian government, army, navy and air force. For each implementation, the effects and bonuses are the same, with the exception of bonus effects that are possible with a successful outcome of the operation. When infiltrated into the administration, agents will try to get into the government by providing you with information about civil affairs in the country. When implemented in the Army, Navy and Air Force, the data will be on these sections of espionage, respectively. A bonus outcome could be gaining experience from one of the types of troops or political power.

Counterintelligence is used to disrupt the work of enemy agents. The higher this value, the more likely it is that you will be able to stop other people's agents, and the more time and money it will take to carry out an operation against you. Just like any other methods of reconnaissance, they are carried out with the help of agents. For counterintelligence, one agent is enough to carry out the task while in your or an allied country.

And don't forget: New enemies intelligence saves your soldiers from death and gives them new appartunities to live in piece after war.
